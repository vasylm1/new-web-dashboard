# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-31_

This policy explains how **My Tools Hub** handles your data, in line with the EU
General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Data controller

Vasyl Madei — vasylmadei@gmail.com

## What data is processed

Only the content you actively provide to a tool:

- files you upload (images, PDFs, Office documents, spreadsheets);
- text you type (URLs, QR content, contact details, etc.).

## How it is processed

- Uploads and entered text are held **in memory** only for as long as needed to
  produce your result, then discarded. The app does **not** write your content to
  a database or persistent storage.
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies are used; Streamlit's anonymous
  usage statistics are disabled.

## Legal basis

Processing is based on our **legitimate interest** (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in
providing the tool you request. Entering personal data is never required; if you
do, you provide it voluntarily.

## Third-party services (sub-processors)

Data leaves the app **only** when you use a specific tool:

| Tool | Recipient | Data sent |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| PDF → MP3 (File converter) | Google Text-to-Speech (gTTS) | the extracted PDF text |
| YouTube → Markdown | YouTube / Google | the YouTube URL |
| Currency converter | open.er-api.com | the currency codes you pick |

These providers may process data **outside the EU/EEA** (e.g. in the USA).

## Hosting & international transfers

The app is hosted on **Streamlit Community Cloud** (operated by Snowflake Inc.,
**USA**). Request data and server logs — which may include **IP addresses**
(personal data) — are therefore processed in the United States, so there is **no
EU data residency**. This is a personal, non-commercial project.

## Retention

None beyond your session — the app stores nothing after the result is returned.

## Your rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and
data portability, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Because
nothing is retained after your session, there is normally no stored data to act
on. To exercise a right, contact **vasylmadei@gmail.com**.
